Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, our website offers free access to many basic courses and learning materials. However, some advanced courses and premium features may require a subscription.
3. Do I need to create an account?
You can browse some content without an account. However, creating an account allows you to track your progress, access personalized recommendations, and unlock additional features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can improve your vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, completing quizzes, learning new words with examples, and reviewing previous lessons regularly.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we provide certificates for selected courses after successful completion and passing the final assessment.
